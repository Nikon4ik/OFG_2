How online
training works

At Online Fitness Games you purchase a monthly
subscription to the best version of yourself.
You will have a personal training program, nutrition,
team recommendations and coach support

We select a coach and set goals
considering your wishes, tasks, past experience and other details: health, nutrition, deadlines, restrictions

We draw up a training program for a month
considering your form, goals, level of difficulty, and the planned result for this month

We give a training plan and instructions:
you get a detailed description of all exercises with a video (see an example), a diet with a detailed menu for your goals: lose weight, gain weight, get relief

We support, advise and motivate
in a closed community of participants and in personal communication with your coach, also we send checklists, instructions, advice about nutrition, sports and a healthy lifestyle
